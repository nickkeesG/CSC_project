What is your accuracy if you take a majority vote from the crowd?
What is your accuracy if you select your most accurate member, the expert, and
 let them decide? 

expert_vs_crowd_sim.py :
    answers this by simulating this for different crowd sizes
expert_vs_crowd.py :
    answers this by mathematically deriving the probability of a correct vote
    (note that the expert probability is an approximation)

If you play with this widget you will notice that:

When mean accuracy is low, and std is high, the expert does best
When mean accuracy is high, and std is low, the crowd does best
